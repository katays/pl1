BIKING TORONTO'S LAKESHORE

From One Beach to Another With a Park Along the Way!

This map shows a scenic route that can be biked along Toronto's lakeshore. It starts at Sugar Beach near the main part of Toronto's downtown area. The first stop on the route is to Tommy Thompson park, where you can ride by or bike through and the ride ends at Woodbine beach. The estimated time for the length of the ride is about a 20 minute trip.
